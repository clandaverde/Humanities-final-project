Who the Maya's are:

The Maya are indigenous people who reside in Mexico and many countries in Central America such as Guatemala, Belize, El Salvador and Honduras. The term Maya actually comes from Mayapan which was an ancient city in Yucatan located in Mexico. Many to this day believe that the Mayas vanished completely and while that is partly true as you will see theres still some Mayan civilizations that exist even today.
